Rice Village Farmers Market & Weekend Events Guide

January 15, 2026 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

Weekends in Rice Village have a rhythm all their own. The pace slows, the patios fill, and the neighborhood buzzes with markets, brunches, and community events. Here's how to make the most of your weekend in the Village.

What Is the Rice Village Farmers Market?

Every Saturday morning, the Rice Village area comes alive with fresh produce, artisan goods, and the aroma of freshly baked bread. Local farmers and producers set up their stalls, offering everything from heirloom tomatoes and Gulf Coast honey to handmade soaps and fresh-cut flowers.

What You'll Find

  • Fresh produce: Seasonal fruits and vegetables from Texas farms
  • Local meats: Grass-fed beef, free-range chicken, and fresh Gulf seafood
  • Baked goods: Sourdough bread, croissants, cookies, and pies
  • Artisan products: Hot sauces, jams, honey, olive oils, and pickles
  • Flowers: Beautiful seasonal arrangements from local growers
  • Prepared foods: Breakfast tacos, tamales, crepes, and fresh juices

What Is the Best Saturday Morning Routine in Rice Village?

  1. 8:00 AM: Arrive early for the best selection at the farmers market
  2. 9:30 AM: Coffee at Siphon Coffee (see our caffeine crawl) — reward yourself for being an early bird
  3. 10:00 AM: Browse the Saturday morning pop-up vendors along University Blvd
  4. 11:00 AM: Brunch at Tiny's No. 5 (arrive before the rush!)

What Should You Do on a Sunday in Rice Village?

Sundays are for slow mornings and long brunches. Rice Village restaurants pull out all the stops with their Sunday menus — bottomless mimosas, elaborate brunch spreads, and the kind of leisurely meals that stretch past noon.

Sunday Best

  • Brunch at your favorite Village spot
  • Afternoon stroll through the Menil campus (see our Menil cultural day)
  • Window shopping along Rice Blvd
  • Sunset happy hour on a patio

What Weekend Events Happen Regularly in Rice Village?

  • First Thursday Art Walk (monthly) — See our full guide
  • Seasonal festivals: Rice Village hosts seasonal events for major holidays and Houston celebrations
  • Live music: Several bars and restaurants feature live music on weekend evenings
  • Fitness events: Saturday morning run clubs and yoga in the park

Rice Village weekends are about slowing down and savoring the best of Houston. Whether you're shopping the farmers market or lingering over brunch, the Village is where weekends are done right.
