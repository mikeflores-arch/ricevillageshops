Houston Food Festivals: What's Happening Near Rice Village in 2026

January 5, 2026 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

Houston is a food city, and the festival calendar proves it. From massive citywide events to intimate neighborhood tastings, there's always something delicious happening near Rice Village in 2026.

What Are the Major Food Festivals in Houston for 2026?

Houston Livestock Show & Rodeo BBQ Cook-Off (February-March)

The World's Championship Bar-B-Que Contest kicks off rodeo season with hundreds of teams competing for barbecue glory. While the cook-off happens at NRG Park, Rice Village restaurants celebrate with their own BBQ specials (see our rodeo dining guide) and smoked-meat menus.

Houston Restaurant Weeks (August-September)

The city's premier dining event, with prix-fixe menus at top restaurants. Multiple Rice Village restaurants participate — see our full guide for details.

Houston Wine & Food Week (April)

A week-long celebration of Houston's culinary scene featuring wine dinners, chef collaborations, and grand tastings. Many events take place in or near the Rice Village area.

Original Greek Festival (October)

Held at the Annunciation Greek Orthodox Cathedral, not far from Rice Village, this beloved festival celebrates Greek food, music, and culture. The baklava alone is worth the trip.

What Food Events Happen Near Rice Village?

Rice Village First Thursday Art Walk (Monthly)

Many participating restaurants offer special food-and-art pairings (see our Art Walk guide) during the monthly art walk. It's a great way to sample new dishes in a festive atmosphere.

Upper Kirby Wine Walk (Spring & Fall)

Just north of Rice Village, the Upper Kirby District hosts wine walks where local restaurants and shops pour tastings along the route. An easy walk or short rideshare from the Village.

Rice Village Summer Sip & Shop (June)

An annual summer event where Rice Village shops and restaurants collaborate to offer tastings, samples, and special discounts. It's a great way to discover new favorites (see our latest openings) in the neighborhood.

What Is the 2026 Houston Food Festival Calendar?

  • February: Rodeo BBQ Cook-Off + Rice Village rodeo specials
  • April: Houston Wine & Food Week events
  • June: Rice Village Summer Sip & Shop
  • August-September: Houston Restaurant Weeks
  • October: Original Greek Festival
  • Monthly: First Thursday Art Walk food pairings

Houston's food festival scene is unmatched, and Rice Village puts you right in the middle of the action. Keep this calendar handy and start planning your foodie adventures for 2026!
