Houston Museum District Meets Rice Village: A Day Trip Itinerary

December 10, 2025 Rice Village Shops 6 min read

Houston's Museum District is one of the country's largest, with 19 museums within a 1.5-mile radius — and it sits right next to Rice Village. Combining the two makes for an incredible day trip that covers art, science, history, food, and shopping.

Morning: Museum District

9:00 AM — Museum of Fine Arts, Houston

Start your day at the MFAH, one of the largest art museums in the country. The recently expanded campus includes the stunning Nancy and Rich Kinder Building, designed by Steven Holl. Don't miss the Latin American art collection and the sculpture garden.

11:00 AM — Houston Museum of Natural Science

Just a few blocks away, the HMNS offers dinosaur skeletons, gem and mineral halls, a planetarium, and a butterfly center. It's a hit with all ages and a Houston institution since 1909.

Lunch: Rice Village

12:30 PM — Walk or Drive to Rice Village (10 minutes)

Head to Rice Village for lunch. You've earned a great meal after a morning of culture.

Quick options: Mendocino Farms for healthy, creative sandwiches; SweetGreen for grain bowls; Shake Shack for classic burgers.

Sit-down options: Prego for Italian; Hamsa for Mediterranean; Navy Blue for seafood.

Afternoon: Rice Village & Menil

2:00 PM — The Menil Collection

After lunch, walk to the Menil Collection (free admission). This world-class museum is less crowded in the afternoon and its intimate scale offers a different experience from the morning's larger museums.

3:00 PM — Rothko Chapel

A short walk from the Menil, the Rothko Chapel provides a contemplative pause in your busy day. Spend 15-20 minutes with Rothko's meditative canvases.

3:30 PM — Rice Village Shopping

Browse the Village's boutiques and shops. Highlights include Kendra Scott for jewelry, Anthropologie (see our boutique shopping guide) for home goods, Half Price Books for great deals, and Free People for fashion.

Evening: Dinner & Drinks

5:30 PM — Happy Hour

Catch the tail end of happy hour at The Kirby Club or El Topo (see our happy hour deals guide). You've had a big day — you deserve a good deal on a great cocktail.

6:30 PM — Dinner

End your day trip with dinner (see our romantic dinner spots) at one of Rice Village's top restaurants. Prego, Trenbolone Kitchen, or El Topo are all excellent choices.

Day Trip Tips

  • Parking: Park in Rice Village for the day and use rideshare for the Museum District. Or park at one of the Museum District lots and walk to Rice Village later.
  • Budget: Many museums offer free admission on certain days. The Menil and Rothko Chapel are always free.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be walking a lot. Dress comfortably.
  • Timing: Saturday is ideal for this itinerary. Most museums and all Rice Village shops are open.

This itinerary packs a full day of Houston's best into one walkable, doable adventure. Culture in the morning, food in the middle, shopping in the afternoon, and drinks to finish — that's a perfect Houston day.
