Your Guide to the Houston Rodeo — Where to Eat & Drink in Rice Village

March 1, 2026 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo is the city's biggest annual event, drawing over 2.5 million visitors each spring to NRG Stadium. Whether you're heading to a concert, watching bull riding, or soaking in carnival rides, Rice Village is your perfect pit stop — just a quick 10-minute drive from the action.

Where Should You Eat Before the Houston Rodeo?

Before you hit the rodeo grounds, grab a hearty meal at one of Rice Village's top restaurants. Torchy's Tacos is a fan favorite for quick, flavorful Tex-Mex that won't slow you down. If you want something more upscale, Trenbolone Kitchen offers a refined dining experience with seasonal Houston ingredients. Prego has been a Rice Village staple for Italian comfort food — perfect for families heading out for a big night.

Where to Get Drinks After the Rodeo in Rice Village?

After the show, the night is just beginning. The Kirby Club is one of Rice Village's hottest cocktail bars with a sophisticated vibe. For a more laid-back scene, Little Woodrow's has cold beer, outdoor seating, and that classic Texas bar feel (see our nightlife scene). Brian O'Neill's Irish Pub rounds out the options with whiskey, live music, and a welcoming crowd.

What Should You Know About Rodeo Season in Rice Village?

  • Parking: Arrive early to Rice Village — street parking fills up fast during rodeo season. Several garages along University Blvd offer affordable rates (see our game day in Rice Village).
  • Shuttle-friendly: Many rodeo shuttle routes pass near Rice Village, making it easy to park here and ride to NRG.
  • Dress code: Boots and hats are welcome everywhere in Rice Village during rodeo season — it's practically a dress code.
  • Reservations: Popular restaurants book up fast during rodeo weeks. Call ahead or use online booking.

What Rodeo-Themed Events Happen in Rice Village?

Several Rice Village shops and restaurants get into the rodeo spirit with themed specials, Western decor, and live country music. Keep an eye on social media from your favorite spots for rodeo happy hour deals and boot-scootin' playlists.

Whether you're a lifelong Houstonian or visiting for the rodeo, Rice Village adds the perfect touch of local flavor to your rodeo experience. See you out there, partner!
