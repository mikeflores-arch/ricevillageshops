Holiday Shopping Guide: Unique Gifts at Rice Village Boutiques

February 5, 2026 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

Forget the crowded malls and generic gift cards. Rice Village is Houston's premier destination for thoughtful, unique holiday gifts from independent boutiques and beloved local shops.

Where Can You Find Unique Holiday Gifts in Rice Village?

Kendra Scott

The Austin-born jewelry brand's Rice Village location is a holiday shopping essential. Their signature Color Bar lets you customize jewelry pieces — earrings, necklaces, and bracelets in your recipient's favorite colors and metals. It's personal, beautiful, and always a hit.

Free People

For the bohemian soul on your list, Free People offers curated clothing, accessories, and home goods that feel special and on-trend. Their holiday collections feature cozy sweaters, statement jewelry, and gift sets perfect for stocking stuffers.

Anthropologie

From whimsical home decor to artisan candles and unique cookbooks, Anthropologie is a wonderland of giftable finds. Their Rice Village store is beautifully merchandised for the holidays, making browsing half the fun (see our grab a coffee between shops).

Warby Parker

Stylish eyewear makes a surprisingly great gift. Warby Parker offers gift cards, sunglasses, and their signature try-at-home program. The Rice Village showroom staff will help you find the perfect frames.

Half Price Books

For the reader, the music lover, or the collector, Half Price Books is a treasure trove. From rare first editions to vinyl records, you'll find unique gifts at prices that leave room for stocking stuffers.

James Avery

This Texas jewelry institution has been a holiday gift go-to for generations. Their charm bracelets, rings, and pendants carry meaningful symbolism and exceptional craftsmanship.

What Are the Best Holiday Shopping Tips for Rice Village?

  • Shop early: Rice Village boutiques stock limited quantities of unique items. Don't wait until Christmas Eve.
  • Gift wrapping: Many Village shops offer complimentary gift wrapping during the holidays.
  • Small Business Saturday (see our weekend events): The Saturday after Thanksgiving is a big day in Rice Village with special promotions and events.
  • Parking: Holiday weekends get busy. Consider weekday evening shopping for a more relaxed experience.
  • Gift cards: When in doubt, most Rice Village shops sell gift cards. Let your loved one choose their own treasure.

This holiday season, give gifts that tell a story. Rice Village boutiques make it easy to find something special for everyone on your list.
