Brunch in Rice Village: The Ultimate Weekend Guide

December 5, 2025 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

Weekend brunch is a religion in Houston, and Rice Village is home to some of the city's best temples of late-morning indulgence. Whether you're craving eggs Benedict, avocado toast, or bottomless mimosas, the Village has you covered.

Where Is the Best Brunch in Rice Village?

Tiny's No. 5

The reigning brunch champion of Rice Village. Tiny's weekend brunch menu features elevated classics — think lemon ricotta pancakes, smoked salmon Benedict, and a brunch burger that has its own fan club. The mimosa carafe is a must-order, and the sidewalk patio is prime real estate on sunny mornings (see our best patios).

SweetGreen

For the health-conscious bruncher, SweetGreen's grain bowls and fresh salads are a guilt-free way to start the weekend. It's quick, it's nutritious, and it leaves you energized rather than comatose. Perfect when you have afternoon plans.

Mendocino Farms

Creative sandwiches and salads that go beyond basic brunch fare. Their weekend specials often feature seasonal ingredients and inventive combinations. The vibe is casual but the food is anything but.

Tout Suite

If pastries are your brunch love language, Tout Suite speaks fluently. Fresh croissants, macarons, artisan coffee, and beautiful plating make this café a must for the Instagram brunch crowd. The airy interior is gorgeous for a leisurely morning.

Torchy's Tacos

Breakfast tacos are Houston brunch at its most authentic. Torchy's morning menu includes scrambled egg tacos, chorizo and potato, and the legendary Trailer Park — a fried chicken strip taco with green chiles and queso. It's messy, it's delicious, it's Houston.

What Is the Rice Village Brunch Crawl?

Can't decide? Do a mini brunch crawl:

  1. Start: Coffee and a pastry at Tout Suite
  2. Main course: Brunch plate at Tiny's No. 5
  3. Dessert: Cookie from Insomnia Cookies or ice cream from Creamistry
  4. Nightcap (morning cap?): Bloody Mary at Little Woodrow's (see our happy hour deals)

What Are the Best Brunch Tips for Rice Village?

  • Arrive early: The 10-11 AM window fills up fast at popular spots. Arrive by 10 for the best experience.
  • Reservations: Tiny's and other sit-down spots accept weekend brunch reservations. Use them.
  • Weekday brunch: Some Rice Village spots serve brunch on weekdays too, with much shorter waits.
  • Outdoor seating: Spring and fall mornings are perfect for patio brunch (see our summer dining tips). Summer mornings work too if you're there before 11.

Rice Village brunch is a weekly ritual for many Houstonians. Join the tradition and discover your new favorite Saturday morning spot.
