Happy Hour Guide: Best Deals at Rice Village Bars

December 30, 2025 Rice Village Shops 5 min read

After a long day, nothing hits like a great happy hour deal. Rice Village bars and restaurants compete to offer the best after-work specials, and the winners are the customers. Here's your definitive guide to happy hour in the Village.

Best Happy Hour Deals

Little Woodrow's

When: Monday-Friday, 3-7 PM

The deal: Discounted buckets of domestic beer, $2 off craft drafts, and half-price select appetizers. Their patio is the ultimate happy hour destination when the weather cooperates.

The Kirby Club

When: Tuesday-Friday, 4-7 PM

The deal: $8 signature cocktails (normally $14-16), $6 wines by the glass, and a special happy hour bites menu. This is upscale happy hour at its finest.

Brian O'Neill's Irish Pub

When: Monday-Friday, 3-7 PM

The deal: $4 well drinks, $5 pints of Guinness, and half-price Irish nachos. The warm pub atmosphere makes this feel like a genuine happy hour, not just a discount.

D&T Drive Inn

When: Daily, 2-7 PM

The deal: Some of the cheapest drinks in Rice Village, period. Their daily specials rotate, but you can always count on affordable pitchers and a fun, dive-bar atmosphere.

El Topo

When: Monday-Friday, 3-6 PM

The deal: $6 margaritas, $5 Mexican beers, and half-price queso and guacamole. Their patio is the spot for after-work margaritas.

Happy Hour Strategy

The Bar Hop

Start at D&T for cheap drinks, walk to Brian O'Neill's for a pint, and finish at The Kirby Club for a craft cocktail. You'll have hit three great bars without breaking the bank.

The Food-Focused Happy Hour

El Topo's half-price apps → Little Woodrow's appetizer specials → share plates at The Kirby Club. You'll have dinner covered at happy hour prices.

Pro Tips

  • Tuesday & Wednesday are the least crowded happy hours. Monday is surprisingly busy.
  • Sit at the bar: Many restaurants offer bar-only happy hour menus that aren't available at tables.
  • Arrive early: The best patio spots fill up fast, especially on Fridays.
  • Check social media: Some bars run flash happy hour extensions or secret specials on their Instagram stories.

Rice Village happy hours offer some of the best value in Houston. Whether you're a cocktail connoisseur or a beer-and-wings person, there's a happy hour here with your name on it.
